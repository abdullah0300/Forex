Roadmap

By following these steps and receiving our guidance, you will have a structured path towards becoming a successful forex trader.

Step 1
Forex Basics and Terminology
  • Intro to Market Structure
  • Familiarize with Key Forex Terminologies
Step 2
Risk Management
  • Calculate Position Sizes
  • Set Stop-Loss Orders
  • Develop a Risk Management Plan
Step 3
Technical Analysis
  • Learn to read charts, trend lines, and resistance levels
  • Use indicators to analyze price action
Step 4
Fundamental Analysis
  • Learn the impact of economic news, geopolitical events, and central bank decisions
  • Able to interpret Economic Indicators
Step 5
Trading Strategies
  • Master trend following, breakout trading, and range trading
  • Being able to select a strategy that suits your trading style
Step 6
Trading Plan Development
  • Assistance from mentors in creating a comprehensive trading plan that includes goals, risk tolerance, and preferred trading style
  • Instructors will personally teach specific entry and exit criteria and how to use signals
Step 7
Demo Trading
  • Demo trading account provided by mentors to practice their strategies
  • Analysis by professional traders of their results, and identification of strengths and weaknesses
Step 8
Live Trading with Small Positions
  • Professional guidance to start trading with small positions in a live account
  • We will counsel you to follow a trading plan, and manage emotions
Step 9
Trade Journaling and Analysis
  • Encouragement to maintain a trade journal, record their trades
  • Daily review of journal to identify patterns and areas for improvement
Step 10
Ongoing Education and Adaptation
  • Receive ongoing educational resources, webinars, and updates on market conditions
Step 11
Performance Evaluation and Goal Setting
  • Regular evaluation of clients' trading performance
  • Assistance in setting new goals based on their progress
  • Several high-yeild signals provided throughout the day
